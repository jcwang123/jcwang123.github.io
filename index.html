Jiacheng Wang

I am currently a PhD student at the Department of Computer Science at School of Informatics, Xiamen University, supervised by Professor Liansheng Wang. Before that, I received my Bachelor’s degree at the Department of Computer Science at School of Informatics, Xiamen University in July 2018.


My research interests are in Medical Imaging Processing, including Object Segmentation and Surgery Analysis. I recently work on data-efficient learning such as federated learning and contrastive learning and transformer-based segmentation tasks.
